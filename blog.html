Opening Thoughts...

10/30/2015
Every time you post something online, you have a choice. You can either make it something that adds to the happiness levels in the world - or you can make it something that takes away...There is enough sadness in the world already. You don't need to add to it. - Zoe Sugg

Welcome to the initial installment of my blog! I will try to use this space as an accurate recording of my web development victories and fails. Hopefully, I will be able to look back at these entries and realize just how far I was able to go in six short months.

As I write this, it is Fall of 2015, and my favorite team, the New York Mets, are down 2-0 in the World Series. Much like them, I sometimes feel like an underdog in life in general. On the surface, I probably don't look like a typical web developer, gamer, or classically trained musician, but the cool thing about being an underdog is that you get to surprise the heck out of people pretty often. I pray that my Mets are able to do that, but more importantly I hope that I can reach the goal that I have set for myself of finishing the Bootcamp and securing a job that I really enjoy.
